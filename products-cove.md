## What's in the Box

Every Three Pillar engagement includes a **Cove Document Backup** licence as the foundation layer — ensuring your critical business documents are protected from day one.

### Cove Document Backup

Cove Document Backup provides continuous, cloud-based protection for your business-critical documents and files.
It forms Pillar One of the Three Pillar methodology — the foundational protect layer — and is provisioned automatically as part of every OAS Backup & Recovery engagement.

* Continuous cloud backup of documents and files
* Automated, agentless deployment
* Granular file and folder-level recovery
* Immutable backup storage — ransomware resistant
* Centralised management via Cove dashboard
* Included in standard Three Pillar licence
