## What Adlumin MDR Is — and What It Delivers

Adlumin MDR is a cloud-native security operations platform acquired by N-able in November 2024. It combines SIEM, UEBA, SOAR, and a 24/7 human-analyst SOC in a single managed service — all operated by OAS on your behalf.

The SOC Layer

24/7 Human Analysts + AI

Adlumin SOC analysts monitor your environment around the clock. When a threat is confirmed, they take immediate containment action and deliver a fully investigated incident report — with evidence, root cause, and remediation guidance. You receive answers, not raw alerts.

The Detection Engine

SIEM + UEBA Across Your Full Environment

Adlumin ingests logs from endpoints, cloud platforms, network devices, firewalls, and identity systems in real time. User and Entity Behaviour Analytics (UEBA) baselines normal activity and flags deviations — detecting compromised credentials and insider threats that generate no malware signatures.

Automated Response

SOAR Playbooks — Minutes, Not Hours

Pre-built and customisable playbooks execute containment actions automatically: isolate an endpoint, disable a compromised account, block a malicious IP, or force a password reset. Automation reduces the window between detection and containment — limiting the blast radius of every incident.

## Visibility and Control Across Your Environment

The Adlumin platform gives your OAS-managed environment a suite of security operations tools that go beyond detection — providing visibility into vulnerabilities, identity risks, and network health.

Perimeter Defence

Scans your IP addresses from an external (attacker's) perspective to identify open ports, SSL certificate issues, and known vulnerabilities that present an attack vector. Gives visibility into your external exposure before attackers find it.

Threat Intelligence

Open-source intelligence on malicious IPs, file hashes, threat feeds, and ATIP Shield data. Threat intel feeds can be integrated directly into your firewall configuration to block known-malicious traffic at the perimeter.

Custom Detections

Create or modify query-based rules that trigger when specific log events are matched — enabling detection tailored to your environment, data sources, and risk profile. The OAS SOC team works with you to tune and refine these over time.

Enhanced Monitoring

Track specific use cases by defining scope and severity. Automatically generates tracking detections when matching events occur — providing focused, noise-reduced monitoring for your highest-priority systems and processes.

Network Health Index

A composite score based on 12 network health factors, updated hourly. Provides detailed drill-down views for deeper analysis — giving OAS technicians and your team a fast read on overall network posture.

Customer Dashboard

Your tenant dashboard provides an overview of asset security posture: at-risk systems, at-risk shares and groups, account totals, sensor health, compliance insights, and SIEM event severity distribution — all in one view.

**How data reaches the SOC:** The **Adlumin Agent** is deployed on endpoints to collect and analyse log data, and to receive and execute response commands from the Adlumin portal. The **Adlumin Forwarder** — a customised Ubuntu Linux VM — securely forwards syslog data from firewalls, VPN servers, and other network sources to the Adlumin platform for analysis by the SOC team.

## POPIA Breach Notification in 72 Hours

South African organisations must notify the Information Regulator within 72 hours of a personal information breach. Without continuous monitoring, meeting this obligation is nearly impossible.

Adlumin MDR provides the detection capability, incident documentation, and evidence chain to support POPIA breach notification — and built-in compliance report templates for other regulatory frameworks applicable to your industry.
