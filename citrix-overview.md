## Everything Citrix — One Predictable Annual Subscription

Introduced in March 2024, UHMC replaced every previous Citrix standalone product and licensing model with a single SKU. It ended the era of balancing desired functionality against available budget — every UHMC customer receives Premium Edition entitlement across the full product set, with the flexibility to run Citrix in a customer-managed data centre, in Citrix Cloud, or in a hybrid of both under the same licence.

One Licence Covers

The Entire Citrix Product Set — Premium Edition

UHMC includes Citrix DaaS (virtual apps and desktops), Citrix Virtual Apps and Desktops for on-premises, Secure Private Access for ZTNA, NetScaler ADC Premium (unlimited virtual instances, up to 1000 Gbps), XenServer Premium Edition, uberAgent for UXM and endpoint security analytics, DeviceTrust for device context, Citrix Analytics for Performance and Security, and Citrix Endpoint Management. **No separate SKUs, no tier upgrades, no feature unlocks.**

How Licensing Works

Concurrent Users — Deploy Anywhere

UHMC is licensed per concurrent user — the number of users actively connected at any one time, not the total user population. This model is ideal for organisations where not all staff are online simultaneously. The same licence entitles you to run workloads **on your own infrastructure, in Citrix Cloud, in Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, or across all of them simultaneously** — without changing licence or paying more.

Why It Matters

30–40% Reduction in Total IT Spend

Organisations consolidating on UHMC typically reduce total IT spending by 30–40% by eliminating separate licensing relationships for load balancers, hypervisors, monitoring tools, and security analytics platforms. The unlimited NetScaler entitlement alone often justifies the UHMC investment — organisations that previously purchased NetScaler per-instance find they can **deploy additional NetScaler capacity across their entire estate at no additional licence cost.**

On-Premises Deployments

What About Citrix CVAD — On-Premises?

**Citrix Virtual Apps and Desktops (CVAD)** is the on-premises equivalent of Citrix DaaS — it uses many of the same components (VDAs, Delivery Controllers, StoreFront, Citrix Studio, NetScaler Gateway) but the control plane is managed and hosted by your organisation rather than by Citrix in the cloud. CVAD remains a valid and widely deployed architecture, particularly for organisations with strict data residency requirements, air-gapped environments, or where an existing on-premises investment is still delivering value.
  
  
Under UHMC, customers are entitled to run Citrix in a customer-managed environment, in Citrix Cloud, or in a hybrid of both — so existing CVAD deployments can continue operating under the new licence while a phased migration to DaaS is planned. If you are running a legacy CVAD environment or evaluating whether to remain on-premises or transition to Citrix Cloud, OAS can provide architecture guidance, component maps, licensing comparisons, and a migration roadmap tailored to your organisation.
